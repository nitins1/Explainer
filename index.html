What we've done

Possessions

A digital safeguard for all of your things

Possessions needed a video to explain what their app does. As a new technology, it was hard for viewers to understand how their service could benefit them. Their tracking devices were being produced, their app was being developed, but their idea needed to get out there. We created a video that communicated the goal of their innovative technology without any confusing engineering jargon.

BackPack 'Em

A University marketplace

BackPack ‘Em was stepping into the UMBC market with an app that let students sell their things to other students. They prioritized safety, security, and a student-first mission but wanted to sell more than just books. They were a solution to the things you need for school but also for fun. Tickets, electronics, and clothes were just a few of the things you could sell with BackPack ‘Em. We helped them rebrand their website, app, and created a video that explained what BackPack ‘Em can truly offer.

PaRaBaL

Mobile security for the enterprise

Business was once done on paper. Now, it’s not even done on the desktop computer. Businesses were starting to prioritize mobile technology to expand their reach into new markets and with that came the importance of mobile security. PaRaBal was at the forefront of this new industry and needed a video to tell the decision makers at these organizations why mobile security was something they needed to be ahead of.
